Why Create an Account?

Creating an account lets you keep your schedule, family plans, and task lists in one place instead of scattered across random notes and apps. Once you are signed in, you can come back to the same layout every day without rebuilding your system from scratch.

Think of this login as a doorway to a calm, organized version of your week. Whether you use My Planner for school, work, family, or all three, signing in makes it easier to pick up where you left off and keep moving forward.

Your information on this sample site is not truly stored, but this page represents how a real login screen would work. In a live app, your account would keep your settings, saved plans, and preferences so that every device could show the same organized view of your life.
