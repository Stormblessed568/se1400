Welcome

Sign in to access your planner and keep everything in sync.

When you sign in, this sample planner imagines how a real account would work: your schedule, family plans, and to-dos could stay in one place instead of scattered across different apps. Each time you return, you would see the same layout, saved tasks, and preferred filters so you can get right back to planning instead of rebuilding everything.

This sample login is part of the My Planner project and is meant to show what a real app could look like. In a full version you could save your weekly schedule, keep family plans together, and come back to the same organized view from any device.
