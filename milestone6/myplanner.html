How It All Fits Together

Tips for Getting Started

Begin by choosing one main priority for each day. Ask yourself, "If I only get one important thing done today, what should it be?" Put that at the top of your Today section. Everything else is extra progress.

Use the Day View when you want structure and the Week View when you feel overwhelmed. Looking at the whole week often shows you that you have more time than you think, just not all on the same day. This makes it easier to spread out big tasks like projects, studying, or deep cleaning.

Review and Reset

At the end of the day, quickly check off what you finished and circle anything that needs to move to tomorrow. This should take only a minute or two. On Sundays, glance at your Goals Board and adjust your week so your time matches what actually matters to you, not just what feels urgent in the moment.
