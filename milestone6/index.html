Welcome to My Planner

Keep your schedule, family plans, and to-dos in one simple place so you can focus on what matters most instead of trying to remember everything.

Why Use My Planner?

Take Control of Your Time

Turn your busy days into a clear, simple plan. Use My Planner to block out time for classes, work, workouts, and rest so everything has a place. When you can see your day at a glance, it's easier to say “yes” to the right things and “no” to the distractions.

Sync Plans With Your Family

No more guessing who is doing what and when. Share plans with your family so everyone knows about appointments, activities, and important events. When schedules are visible to everyone, it's easier to coordinate rides, chores, and quality time together.

Plan Months in Advance

Look ahead to big deadlines, trips, and goals without losing track of today. My Planner helps you map out busy weeks, semesters, or seasons so you're prepared instead of rushed. With a clear long-term view, you can spread out your workload and protect time for the things that matter.
